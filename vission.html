VISION & MISSION


To become a Center of Excellence in Information Technology and Knowledge Management for a more proactive and responsive extension service through innovation, dedicated research and development teams and management expertise tied with a strong technology focus.

Our mission is to meet the needs of IT Industry through effective learning, teaching and development of skills:

 Our Five Key Themes linked to the Mission and Vision

  • Putting better learning and teaching at the heart of what we do.
  • Creating a truly training atmosphere.
  • Getting better at "Putting Learners and Industry First".
  • Leading (leadership not management), and
  • Building the right facilities and human resources we need to do the teaching and learning better.
