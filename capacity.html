Capacity



Currently BITM has a capacity to train over 2,000 professionals (both entry level & management level). We are in the process of increasing the infrastructural capacity to train up to 5000 students in a year. Also we are expanding our presence in all division of Bangladesh. BITM has Twelve (12) well equipped air conditioned training labs, one executive training room, one conference room, one meeting room and a large auditorium.
